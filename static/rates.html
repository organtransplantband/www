Rates

Rates will vary depending on the size of the venue and the amount of PA system equipment required to cover that venue. Seasonal rate adjustments will apply to holiday seasons.

The base rate for the band is $900 for a 3-hour event. One additional hour may be obtained for an additional $300.

The base rate covers four musicians and a vocalist and includes a PA system suitable for small gatherings of up to 100 guests.

Medium sized venues with 100 – 1000 guests will require additional PA system equipment. A 6000 watt JBL / Crown professional grade PA system at $450 is recommended for these events.
Record Your Event

A wonderful gift to give as a memento of your special occasion is a CD recording of the event. Your event can be recorded live to a Mac computer and converted to CD format. This service is only available if the professional grade PA system rental is selected. This is because the recording equipment is integrated into that system.

Typically a 3-hour event results in around 2 hours of material fitting onto a 2-CD set. Ten 2-CD sets are furnished for the base record option fee. Additional sets will be quoted on request. The fee for recording an event with ten 2-CD sets is $350.
